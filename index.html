About Me

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications at Netaji Subhash University, with a strong interest in cybersecurity and penetration testing. My technical expertise includes Linux system operations, web application security, IoT and hardware pentesting, as well as practical experience with network security and threat analysis.
